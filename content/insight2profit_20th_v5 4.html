Pop Quiz
How do you explain the AI execution layer in plain, non-technical language to a client?
One way to say it We use AI to make our models easy to interact with. You ask in plain language or type a request, and the AI execution layer resolves your inputs and acts on them. The whole interaction can be conversational.
Pop Quiz
If a client is already building agents to manage quoting workflows, how can we help them?
One way to say it Their agents handle the workflow; we supply the model that makes the decisions smart — the price recommendation, the conviction behind it, the market context. And if a client already has an agent running in their environment, they can connect it to our model directly via MCP.
